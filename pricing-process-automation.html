Scope Drivers

What Shapes the Price

These are the key factors we consider when scoping a process automation project.

Workflow Complexity

Linear, single-step processes differ significantly from multi-branch workflows with conditional logic and exception handling paths.

Systems & Integrations

The number of applications, APIs, and data sources your automation needs to interact with determines integration scope.

Intelligence Requirements

Rule-based automation differs from AI-assisted workflows requiring natural language processing, document understanding, or decision models.

Volume & Frequency

High-volume, time-critical automations require more robust infrastructure and error handling than low-frequency batch processes.

Compliance & Audit Trails

Regulated industries requiring audit logs, data retention policies, or compliance controls add scope to the implementation.
