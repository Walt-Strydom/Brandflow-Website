Our Process

How We Price

We believe in transparent, fair pricing. Here's how we arrive at a number.

1. Discovery Call

We start with a free consultation to understand your application requirements, user flows, integrations, and technical constraints. No commitment required.

2. Scope & Proposal

We map out the full architecture — frontend, backend, database, and deployment — then provide a detailed written proposal with a fixed price and timeline.

3. Build & Handover

We build, test, and deploy your application. You receive full code ownership, documentation, and a post-delivery support window at handover.

Scope Drivers

What Shapes the Price

These are the key factors we consider when scoping a full stack development project.

Complexity & Features

The number of pages, user roles, workflows, and features directly determines the scope and cost of your application build.

Database & Backend

Simple CRUD applications differ from complex systems requiring custom query logic, real-time updates, or multi-tenant architecture.

Third-Party Integrations

Payment gateways, authentication providers, email services, and external APIs each add integration scope to the project.

Infrastructure & Deployment

Self-hosted vs. cloud deployment (AWS, Azure, GCP), CI/CD pipelines, and scaling requirements all affect infrastructure costs.

Timeline & Support

Standard versus accelerated delivery timelines and post-launch support requirements are scoped and priced transparently.
