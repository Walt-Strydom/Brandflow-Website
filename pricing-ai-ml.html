Our Process

How We Price

We believe in transparent, fair pricing. Here's how we arrive at a number.

1. Discovery Call

We start with a free consultation to understand your business challenge, data landscape, and desired outcomes. No commitment required.

2. Scope & Proposal

Based on your requirements, we define the project scope, architecture, deliverables, and timeline. You receive a detailed written proposal with a fixed price.

3. Delivery & Support

Once approved, we build, test, and deploy your AI solution. Pricing includes a post-delivery support window for the agreed period.

Scope Drivers

What Shapes the Price

These are the key factors we consider when scoping an AI or ML project.

Model Complexity

Simple classification models differ significantly from deep learning systems, LLM integrations, or custom transformer architectures.

Data Requirements

Data availability, quality, volume, and whether preprocessing or collection pipelines are needed all affect scope significantly.

Integrations

The number of systems your AI model needs to connect with — APIs, databases, CRMs, dashboards — determines integration work required.

Timeline & Priority

Standard delivery timelines versus accelerated deadlines affect resource allocation and pricing accordingly.

Ongoing Support

Post-delivery monitoring, model retraining, and maintenance windows are scoped separately based on your operational needs.
